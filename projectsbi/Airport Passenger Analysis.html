Power Bi Project

Airport Passenger Analysis

An interactive airport passenger dashboard built to analyze global passenger traffic by country, airport, and year through geographic and comparative visual reporting.

Power BI Visualization

Project Overview

This Airport Passenger Analysis dashboard was designed as an interactive reporting solution to explore global passenger traffic patterns across airports, countries, and time periods.

The project presents a high-level overview of aviation activity through important KPIs such as total passengers and total airport count, while also examining the distribution of passenger traffic by country, yearly contribution, and top-performing airports based on passenger volume.

Through map visualizations, comparative bar charts, and summary distributions, the dashboard provides a clear understanding of where passenger activity is concentrated and which airports play the most significant role in overall traffic movement.

A detailed tabular view was also included to support deeper analysis by listing airport rankings, airport names, IATA/ICAO codes, and passenger totals, allowing users to move from visual summaries to more detailed operational insights.

By combining geographic, temporal, and airport-level analysis into one dashboard, the project delivers a structured and data-driven perspective on global air passenger traffic, supporting better understanding, comparison, and performance evaluation.
